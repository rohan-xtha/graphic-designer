02
UI/UX Design · Product Design
MindSathi — Mental Health App
+

An AI-powered mental health support platform designed for students across Nepal and South Asia — addressing one of the most underserved UX spaces in the region.

The design challenge was significant: mental health interfaces need to feel safe, calm, and non-clinical — while remaining modern and engaging for students. I designed the complete visual language: a soft, accessible color system, calming typography, and gentle micro-interactions across the AI chat, mood tracker, breathing exercises, and crisis support pages.

The app was selected for the Harvard Health Hackathon National Finale at NTIC/KU — competing against teams from across Nepal.
